CODEX

It is a hackathon that basically based on Web Application (Application or Website). The total time taken by this event will be of 24 hr. This hackathon is organized by 4th year students under CSES(Computer Science Engineering Society).


There are some points regarding to this event that tell us briefly about this event: -

  1. → Event will be conducted in three phases. Idea discussion, Coding judgement and presentation respectively.

  2. → The problem statement will be provided by us. And based on that problem statement, student will present their ideas in the Hackathon.

  3. → The timing of this event will be 9 AM on that day to 9 AM next day (a one-day hackathon)

  4. → During the Hackathon, participant should be present in campus from 9 AM to 3 PM.

  5. → There must be some amount that participants have to pay to participate in this event.

  6. → The question will be sent to participants just before the day of the Hackathon.

  7. → The participant can participants in team. We believe that in companies, the project is done with the teams and it makes better understanding of each other’s thought.

  8. → The size of team should be minimum of 2 and maximum of 3.

  9. → After the code submission, if the number of team is more than 15 then we sort out the number of teams to 10 by analysing their performance and behaviour.

  10. → The winner from this top 10 will get some cash amount. The estimated cash amount for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd is ₹6000. The cash distribution of this event is:

    1st will get ₹3000
    2nd will get ₹2000
    3rd will get ₹1000.
